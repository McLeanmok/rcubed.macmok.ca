Consign With Us In 3 Easy Steps

  1. Download the Contact

    Download our contract, look over the terms and conditions, and fill it out either digitally, or print it out.

  2. Submit in Store

    Once you have the contact information filled out, bring it in to our store. When you are there, one of our staff members will assist you with creating your consigner profile.

  3. Receive Monthly Payouts

    Every time one of your items sells, you get paid! Payouts are Sundays, and our standard rate is $50.00
