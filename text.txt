How to Consign with us
We love new consignors at the shop. So if you are thinking of making an appointment with us in the future, please read our policies below. Once you have an appointment set up, you will be asked to sign our store contract at the time of your drop off.

Want to Consign with us?
Here is what you need to know…….
* ALL PAYOUT REQUESTS need to be done by email Saturday by 10pm
(rcubedconsignment@gmail.com)
We will not accept requests through Messenger or Instagram *
Consignment rate is: 50% Consignor, 50% store
Consignment time is 4 months “in season”
We take any season, any time
1. All items need to be on current trend, and in excellent used
condition. No stains, no pet hair, no pilling, no damage and freshly
laundered. Please bring folded (no hangers), in a box, bin or reusable bag.
2. You are welcome to your payout once a month by Etransfer.
Minimum payout is $50.00 and they are done every Sunday. Please
email for payout, rcubedconsignment@gmail.com
Etransfers are subject to a $5.00 bank fee, taken off your total. Admin fee $1/month
3. You can use your instore credit at any time, on anything in the
store.
4. You need to make an appt to drop off clothing. No exceptions.
Email rcubedconsignment@gmail.com
5. We reserve the right to:
-determine the selling the price of all the items depending on the age,
condition, brand and style
-change the price of items reflecting its time in the store
-donate any item at any time (all items will be donated at the end of
the consignment term)
Charities that we love and support with our donations are:
SOS, Salvation Army, Big Brothers Big Sisters of CVI and various
other community programs

6. We have a monthly fee of $1.00 that will automatically be deducted from your aco
